What is Carbon Footprint?

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases (including carbon dioxide and methane) that are generated by our actions, such as transportation, electricity consumption, and food choices.

Top Ways to Reduce Your Carbon Footprint

  • Reduce, Reuse, Recycle: Cut down on waste and single-use plastics.
  • Save Energy: Switch to LED bulbs and turn off appliances when not in use.
  • Sustainable Travel: Use public transport, walk, bicycle, or switch to electric vehicles.
  • Green Eating: Incorporate more plant-based meals into your daily diet.
  • Conserve Water: Fix leaks and use water-saving fixtures.
